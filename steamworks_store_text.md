## System Requirements

### Minimum
- **OS**: Windows 10 (64-bit)
- **Processor**: 1 GHz or higher
- **Memory**: 4 GB RAM
- **Graphics**: NVIDIA GeForce Series (**Required**)
- **Storage**: 200 MB available space
- **Additional Notes**: Steam Client must be installed.

### Recommended
- **OS**: Windows 11 (64-bit)
- **Graphics**: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 or higher
- **Storage**: 200 MB available space

### Important Notes
- **NVIDIA GPU is REQUIRED.** AMD / Intel GPUs are not supported (Sorry about that!).
- This software uses the official NVIDIA NVAPI to control display settings.
- We read the Windows Registry to detect Steam status, but we never write to it or do anything malicious. Your data stays yours.
